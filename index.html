Sustainability Through the Ages

Explore Intel's journey through time and how innovation supports a more sustainable future.

Desktop: scroll horizontally • Mobile: stacks vertically • Hover/focus to reveal details

1968

Intel Founded

Intel is founded, laying the groundwork for decades of innovation.

  • Started as NM Electronics; renamed Intel Corporation.
  • Set a long-term innovation trajectory in computing.
  • Early focus: engineering scale and performance.

Hover / focus for details

1971

First Microprocessor

Intel debuts the 4004, accelerating the future of computing devices.

  • Introduced general-purpose compute on a chip.
  • Enabled smaller, more capable systems over time.
  • Set the pace for performance-per-watt progress.

Hover / focus for details

1978

8086 Processor

Launch of the 8086 establishes the x86 architecture’s legacy.

  • Created an architectural foundation used for decades.
  • Enabled ecosystem growth: software + hardware alignment.
  • Performance upgrades compounded over generations.

Hover / focus for details

1985

386 Processor

32-bit architecture unlocks a new era of performance and multitasking.

  • Major step forward in compute capability for PCs.
  • Expanded what software could do at the time.
  • Built momentum for the modern PC era.

Hover / focus for details

2006

Peak GHG Emissions

Intel’s operational greenhouse gas emissions reach a high point.

  • Invests in chemical abatement systems to reduce emissions.
  • Expands renewable energy sourcing over subsequent years.
  • Improves manufacturing efficiency to reverse the trend.

Hover / focus for details

2020

RISE Strategy

Intel launches RISE strategy and 2030 goals to drive progress.

  • RISE = Responsible, Inclusive, Sustainable, Enabling.
  • Targets climate action, water stewardship, and waste reduction.
  • Moves sustainability into measurable, time-bound goals.

Hover / focus for details

2022

Net-Zero By 2040

Commitment to reach net-zero (Scope 1 & 2) by 2040.

  • Long-horizon roadmap with staged milestones.
  • Builds on years of operational environmental initiatives.
  • Requires sustained execution and progress tracking.

Hover / focus for details

2023

Renewable Electricity

Intel reaches 99% renewable electricity usage worldwide.

  • Helps lower operational carbon emissions.
  • Supports progress toward long-term climate goals.
  • Demonstrates scale execution across global operations.

Hover / focus for details

2024

Sustainability Summit

Intel hosts its first Sustainability Summit to align stakeholders.

  • Brings suppliers, government, and industry together.
  • Focuses on next-gen sustainable semiconductor manufacturing.
  • Accelerates collaboration on shared sustainability outcomes.

Hover / focus for details
